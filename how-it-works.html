Sports Arbitrage

Sports arbitrage is a risk-free betting technique that takes advantage of the ability to place proportional bets on each outcome of the same event on different bookmakers so that a return is guaranteed regardless of the outcome.Therefore, a sport arbitrage betting opportunity occurs when two or more bookmakers have sufficiently high odds on different sides of a match, a situation that happens thousands of times every day. Every second, tens of thousands of odds/prices are updated on different bookmaker websites.
